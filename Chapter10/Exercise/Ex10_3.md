
### Exercise 10.3



An object receives two forces. The first is:

$$
\mathbf{f}_1 = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix}
$$

applied at:

$$
\mathbf{r}_1 = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix}
$$

relative to its center of mass. The second is:

$$
\mathbf{f}_2 = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}
$$

applied at:

$$
\mathbf{r}_2 = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ -1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}
$$

relative to its center of mass. What is the total torque and force on the object from these two forces?



## Solution:

### 1. **Total Force**:

The total force $\mathbf{F}$ is the vector sum of the two forces $\mathbf{f}_1$ and $\mathbf{f}_2$.

$$
\mathbf{F} = \mathbf{f}_1 + \mathbf{f}_2
$$

Substitute the given values for $\mathbf{f}_1$ and $\mathbf{f}_2$:

$$
\mathbf{f}_1 = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix}, \quad \mathbf{f}_2 = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}
$$

So, the total force is:

$$
\mathbf{F} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 3 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix}
$$

### 2. **Total Torque**:

The torque $\mathbf{\tau}$ for each force is calculated using the cross product of the position vector $\mathbf{r}$ and the force vector $\mathbf{f}$:

$$
\mathbf{\tau}_1 = \mathbf{r}_1 \times \mathbf{f}_1, \quad \mathbf{\tau}_2 = \mathbf{r}_2 \times \mathbf{f}_2
$$

#### For $\mathbf{\tau}_1$:

$$
\mathbf{r}_1 = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix}, \quad \mathbf{f}_1 = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix}
$$

The cross product is:

$$
\mathbf{\tau}_1 = \begin{vmatrix} \mathbf{i} & \mathbf{j} & \mathbf{k} \\ 0 & -1 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 & 3 \end{vmatrix}
$$
Calculating each determinant:

$$
\mathbf{i}((-1)(3) - (1)(2)) = \mathbf{i}(-3 - 2) = -5\mathbf{i}
$$
Thus,

$$
\mathbf{\tau}_1 = -5\mathbf{i} + \mathbf{j} + \mathbf{k} = \begin{pmatrix} -5 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix}
$$

#### For $\mathbf{\tau}_2$:

$$
\mathbf{r}_2 = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ -1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}, \quad \mathbf{f}_2 = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}
$$

The cross product is:

$$
\mathbf{\tau}_2 = \begin{vmatrix} \mathbf{i} & \mathbf{j} & \mathbf{k} \\ 0 & -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \end{vmatrix}
$$

Calculating each determinant:

$$
\mathbf{\tau}_2 = \mathbf{0}
$$

### 3. **Total Torque**:

The total torque $\mathbf{\tau}$ is the sum of the individual torques:

$$
\mathbf{\tau} = \mathbf{\tau}_1 + \mathbf{\tau}_2
$$

### Final Solution:

- **Total Force**: $\mathbf{F} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 3 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix}$
- **Total Torque**: $\mathbf{\tau} = \begin{pmatrix} -5 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix}$
